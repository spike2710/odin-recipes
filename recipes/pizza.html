PIZZA

Description

A warm and fresh pizza, straight outta the oven

It is made using the freshest ingredients which are sourced from local farmers.

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pizza Dough
  • Pepperoni
  • Basil

Steps to follow:

  1. Strech the pizza dough and spread a good layer of the tomato sauce.
  2. Slice a good amount of pepperoni and spread evenly.
  3. Slide the pizza into the oven for 15 mins and 450C.
