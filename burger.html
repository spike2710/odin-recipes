BURGER

Description

This Burger looks decilious, doesn't it?

It is made using the freshest ingredients which are sourced from local farmers.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Chicken Patty
  • Mustard Sauce

Steps to follow:

  1. Pan fry the burger buns till golden brown.
  2. Cook the chicken patty till brown and crisp on both sides.
  3. Finally assemble in the order of bun, mustard sauce, patty, lettuce and the 2nd bun on top.
